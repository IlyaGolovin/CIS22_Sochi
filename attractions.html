Zhemchuzhina

4-star hotel

The Grand Hotel Zhemchuzhina is located on the territory of 70 000 m2 on the first coastline of the Black Sea, in the central part of the city of Sochi. Zhemchuzhina (Sochi) is a modern 17-storey hotel in the center of Sochi, from which a beautiful panorama of the Caucasus mountains and the Black Sea opens. The hotel is surrounded by a unique park area with tropical plants, next to which there is the "Winter Theater", a circus, a concert hall "Festival", a botanical park "Arboretum", museums and theaters. Affordable prices and comfortable rooms for every taste.

Lazurnaya Hotel & Spa has been welcoming This property is 3 minutes walk from the beach.
